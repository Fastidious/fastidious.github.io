First day

14:00 on Sunday, 11 July 2004

I must admit, before I continue, that I did not exercise last night. At least, not as I had intended. It is my fault, I became lazy and even though Kim pushed me well, it did not worked out. But… I am proud the say that I just walked three miles this morning and, boy, it felt good. I know it is not a sensational “news”, but I wanted to mark the day of my come back. I will be fit in no time. I have to. I must. Next, I will buy a pedometer. Thanks to my friend Martin for the suggestion!

Off topic, my friend George has joined the hurd of weblogers. Previously Gee had done it too, even though he is now “sleeping”. Welcome aboard George!
