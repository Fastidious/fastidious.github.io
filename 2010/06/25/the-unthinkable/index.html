The Unthinkable

Friday, 25 June 2010

Yesterday I did the unthinkable. Something I have never done — nor will I do again — before. I went very early (03:15) to stand at the Millenia all, to be one of the first (or so I thought) to buy the iPhone 4.

I don’t want to write long about something I am more than half way ashamed of. Suffice it to say that I got an iPhone 4 after standing in line for 14 hours (17:15). Never again.

The photo above was taken by George, around 11:30, not even half way my day there. Did I say never again?

I am a father and a husband living in Orlando, FL, U.S.A.
