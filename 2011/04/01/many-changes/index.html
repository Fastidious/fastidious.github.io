Many changes coming soon

14:00 on Friday, 01 April 2011

Just very recently I wrote about me moving to Jekyll from WordPress:

After much thought, and tinkering, I decided to ditch my six year old or so WordPress and start blogging like a hacker with the awesome Jekyll.

I also wrote —fairly recently— about living on a Linode:

Moved to Linode. Love it, highly recommend it. They reward customers that bring referrals, but notice my links do not have any referral code. That much I like their product.

Now, not even two months after, I need to publicly admit:

  • Jekyll isn’t good. I hate it! I am going back to WordPress.
  • Linode is horrible. I have decided instead to host with Godaddy.

And that’s that.
