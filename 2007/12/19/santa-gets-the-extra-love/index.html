Santa gets the extra love

14:00 on Wednesday, 19 December 2007

KM believes in Santa. It is that childish fantasy we do not want to spoil quite yet. But the fat guy, with red cheeks, who pilots a sleigh pulled by reindeers, gets all the extra love –and credits– for the job we, as parents, do during Christmas time. I challenge Santa to come up with a Wii!

1992 — 2021. David Collantes.
