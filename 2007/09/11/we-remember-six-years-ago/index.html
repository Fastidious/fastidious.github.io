We remember: Six years ago

14:00 on Tuesday, 11 September 2007

Today, six years ago, a group of animals belonging to the human race decided to take away thousands of innocent lives by destroying the Twin Towers (see World Trade Center). Let us not to forget how stupid they were, how criminal they were, how inhuman they were. Let us remember our brothers and sisters who died that day. For me The Towers stand tall. They always will.

September 11th, 2001: We will never forget!

↑ 1992 — 2021. David Collantes.
